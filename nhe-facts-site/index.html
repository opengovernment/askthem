Looking Ahead: 2024–2033

Health spending growth is projected to average 5.8% per year through 2033, outpacing GDP growth of 4.3%.

By 2033, healthcare is expected to reach 20.3% of GDP, up from 17.6% in 2023.

Federal healthcare spending is projected to rise from $2.4T (48%) to $4.3T (53%) of total health spending by 2033.
